College is expensive. When will you break even?

Education generally increases salaries. Between tuition costs and years of lost income, however, it might not be as fruitful as you thought. This calculator will estimate the age at which you will break even.

Say you have two options:

  1. Without going to college, you make $20k/yr. Over 10 years, you earn $200000.
  2. You could spend $30k/yr to school to school for 4 years. After graduation, you earn $50k/yr for 6 years. Over the same 10 years, you've earned $180000.
In the scenario above, the person would break even a little after 10 years. When will you break even?
